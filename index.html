LABS

IIITD, an institute leading in education and research relevant to the current technologies and needs of industry, is running several labs to support its teaching and research work being carried out by its faculty members, research scholars and students, in both CSE and ECE streams. All research labs are located in Faculty and Research block while all teaching labs are housed in the Lecture Hall Complex on the campus. Research labs, associated with focused research groups, are working in areas of IT and Electronics, details about which are given here. Teaching labs help students understand theoretical concepts taught in the course of their study.

Digital Circuits Lab

Digital Circuits Lab

DC Lab (301), dedicated to concepts and implementation of basic circuits designing. It serves its facilities as one of the teaching labs and also to facilitate course projects.

Explore Lab
Circuits & Innovation Lab

Circuits & Innovation Lab

CIL Lab (302), dedicated to circuits designing, serves as a teaching lab for teaching projects and research projects including experimental and innovative study in analog/digital circuits and microcontrollers.

Explore Lab
Basic Electronics Lab

Basic Electronics Lab

BE Lab (303), dedicated to concepts and implementation of basic circuits designing. It serves as a teaching lab and also to facilitate course projects for students.

Explore Lab
RF & Applied EM Lab

RF & Applied Electromagnetics Lab

RFAEL lab (304) acts as a research lab for scholars and interns, and as a course lab for thesis projects. Research areas include high frequency devices, antenna theory, and RF/Applied Electromagnetic Engineering.

Explore Lab
Shannon Lab

Shannon Lab

Shannon Lab (305) manages teaching programs with experiments and project assignments, while supporting research scholars, interns and students in signal processing and wireless communication.

Explore Lab
Advanced ECE Lab

Advanced ECE Lab

Supports experimental research in wireless and electronic communication systems, including a 5G testbed, RIS/ORIS setups, D2D communication testbeds, and high-performance workstations for real-time signal processing and prototyping.

Projects


March 2021

Touchless Tap

ECE lab has come up with a new cost-effective product 'Touchless Tap'. To ensure safety during the Corona pandemic, a sensor-based water tap is developed which doesn't require manual operation. It works when it detects your hand under the nozzle. This project is designed and developed by Research Engineers Sana & Khagendra.

September 2022

ELEO

ECE Lab Enquiry Officer (ELEO) is a chatbot which is designed to resolve any queries related to ECE Labs. You can ask the questions and it will assist you regarding the same within seconds. The project is designed by B.Tech student Jayan Pahuja under the guidance of Engineers Sana Ali Naqvi & Khagendra Joshi.

Read More
November 2019

Light Automation

This summer project is aimed at making the ECE-Labs fully automated, with lights being the first target. The project utilized ECE-Lab resources, such as Arduino Microcontroller, PIR sensors, relays etc. This was designed by B.Tech students Paritosh & Amisha under the guidance of Engineer Abhishek Kumar.
